# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making DataPilot a welcoming, professional, and respectful open-source community.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Discuss ideas and code, not people.
- Keep feedback actionable.
- Respect privacy and security boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruptive behavior.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or exploit details publicly.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.
